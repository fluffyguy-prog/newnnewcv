My Expirience as a Summer Intern at MTNL

  • I started as a summer intern at MTNL in the summer of 2019 working under various Deputy Managers and top level officers and finally reporting to Sr Manager of MTNL,Powai,Mumbai.

  • I worked at Optical Cable Fiber Department i.e PCM center of the local MTNL telephone exchange.

  • Duration of this internship was 1 month.

Brief outlook of what I learnt at MTNL

  1. For the first 4 days of our internship I was trained to operate on Packet Tracer and given a brief introduction of how a telephone exchange works
  2. I was then trained to model broadband system and then trained on mpls routers too.
  3. I was also taken on a field trip were I was shown how cable laying is done and how its spliced and assembled before its laid inside the ground
  4. I was also given a detailed training on how to handle the Network Management System
  5. In the end I had a to create a detailed report about my study and learnings at MTNL
